Q Energy Dashboard

An active management of your carbon emissions. Energy usage and carbon reduction monitored easily through the QEnergy™ Dashboard.

Overview

The Dashboard provides:
• Target setting and tracking system to monitor carbon reduction actions
• Monthly report with energy and cost saving insights
• Daily/weekly energy usage prediction and insights from historical and real-time data
• Access to energy usage and billing data in one place for all meters and sites
• Validation of your bills with energy bill breakdown and reporting

Problem

Currently, in the UK only 45% of the electricity bill is made up of actual energy costs, the rest of it is made of various charges based on when you use the energy and government levies to keep the network in balance. This situation is set to get worse as the UK tries to meet decarbonisation targets through existing centralised grid infrastructure, along with the growth of renewables, electric vehicles and electric heating. This is a global challenge currently being addressed by countries such as the US, UK, Australia and others in Western Europe.

Empathize/Define

Understanding user needs:
After analyzed the competition and realized what users need to save on their energy consumption, We have found that many users have a huge difficulty controlling their energy consumption at home, large families have more difficult as well regarding the number of children / young people living at home who have uncontrolled consumption for example: when they forget about the lights on all the time, showers for a long time, playing console games and watching TV for a long time, this is a result of the imbalance based on the consumption of electricity, namely the people who spend more time at home as house-made, consecutive washing of clothes/dishes also they generating a large consumption of electricity that is not controlled.
